    • Teach Brian to Code

    Meet Brian. He is 3 years old puggle (Pug and Beaggle cross) and he loves to chasing moving objects(especially cats!), learn new stuff and COOKIES!

    One day at the dog play ground, Brian heard that there is a website teaches cat to code (Yes. You heard me right, a CAT!). He couldn't believe there is such a thing (but after all, cats are smart creatures and he recall he watched youtube video that a cat plays piano). He asked me if I can find a website teaches dog to code. After several hours of researching, I told him that unfortunately there is no such a thing. Brian smiled at me and said I should start one.

    As I am starting new journey of learning programming languages, I think it will be fun to keep the record on progress. This may help others like Brian who wants to start learning.

    In case you want to know little bit about me, I am a Korean-Canadian living in California. Other than learning programining languages, I spend my time with Brian, brewing beer(or apple cyder) or developing a new vegetarian recipies. You can read more about me here
